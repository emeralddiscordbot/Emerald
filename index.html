Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how Emerald Bot ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information we collect

  • Server data: Server ID, name, channels, and configuration.
  • User data: User IDs, usernames, and discriminators for moderation and leveling.
  • Message content: Limited scanning for moderation (bad words, spam).
  • Usage data: Command usage, feature interactions, and error logs.
  • Configuration: Your custom settings and preferences.

2. How we use your information

  • Provide and maintain core bot functionality.
  • Improve and personalize the experience.
  • Monitor abuse and enforce our Terms.
  • Respond to support requests and troubleshoot.
  • Develop new features.

3. Data security

We use encryption, secure servers, and strict access controls to protect your data.

4. Data retention

  • Server data: Until the bot is removed from your server.
  • User data: For as long as you are a member of a server using Emerald.
  • Moderation logs: 90 days for dispute handling.
  • Analytics: Anonymized for up to 2 years.

5. Third‑party services

Emerald only interacts with Discord's API. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

6. Your rights

  • Access, rectify, or delete your data.
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Contact us through our support server to exercise these rights.

7. Children's privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from them.

8. Automated decisions

Emerald uses automated moderation. You may request human review by contacting us.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact us

Via our support server.
