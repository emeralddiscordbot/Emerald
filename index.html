How do I add Emerald Bot to my server?

Simply click the "Invite to Server" button on our website. You'll be redirected to Discord's authorization page where you can select your server and grant the necessary permissions.

What permissions does Emerald Bot need?

Emerald requires basic permissions like reading messages, sending messages, managing messages, and kicking/banning members for moderation features. You can customize permissions during the invite process.

Is Emerald Bot free to use?

Yes, Emerald Bot is completely free with all core features available. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.

How can I get support for Emerald Bot?

Join our Discord support server using the "Support" button on our website. Our community and support team are available 24/7 to help with any questions or issues.

Can I suggest new features for Emerald?

Absolutely! We welcome feature suggestions in our Discord server. We regularly review community feedback and implement popular requests in our updates.

This Privacy Policy explains how Emerald Bot ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Discord bot.

1. Information We Collect

  • Server Information: Server ID, name, configuration settings, and channel IDs necessary for bot functionality
  • User Information: User IDs, usernames, and discriminators for features like moderation, leveling, and welcome messages
  • Message Content: Limited message content for moderation purposes (filtered for inappropriate content) and command processing
  • Usage Data: Command usage statistics, feature interaction data, and error logs to improve our services
  • Configuration Data: Your custom settings, preferences, and bot configurations for your server

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the bot's core functionality
  • To improve and customize the user experience
  • To monitor for abuse and enforce our Terms of Service
  • To respond to user support requests and troubleshoot issues
  • To develop new features and improve existing ones
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our services

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Data Retention

  • Server Data: Retained until the bot is removed from your server
  • User Data: Retained for the duration of your server membership where Emerald is present
  • Moderation Logs: Retained for 90 days to handle disputes and appeals
  • Analytics Data: Retained in anonymized form for up to 2 years

5. Third-Party Services

Emerald Bot only interacts with Discord's API to provide its services. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Your Rights

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: Request temporary restriction of processing
  • Right to Object: Object to our use of your data based on legitimate interests

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

8. Automated Decision-Making

Emerald Bot uses automated processes for moderation features. You have the right to request human intervention or challenge these decisions by contacting us.
